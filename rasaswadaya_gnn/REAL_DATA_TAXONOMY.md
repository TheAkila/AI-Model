## 11. HOW TO GATHER REAL DATA

### Primary Sources:
1. **Rasaswadaya.lk Platform** - Sri Lankan cultural artists database
2. **YouTube Channels** - Artist profiles and performances
3. **Local News** - Event announcements and artist interviews
4. **Social Media** - Instagram (artist_name_sl), Facebook artist pages
5. **Festival Websites** - Event dates, performer lists
6. **University Archives** - Peradeniya, Colombo, Moratuwa

### Data Points to Collect per Artist:
- Full name & stage name
- Primary art form(s)
- Specialty genres
- Regions associated
- Language(s) of performance
- Performance style (traditional/contemporary/fusion)
- Active since (year)
- Current location/base
- Associated groups/companies
- Notable performances & venues
- Social media presence
- Contact information (if available)

### Data Points to Collect per Event:
- Event name
- Date & duration
- Venue & coordinates
- Associated artists
- Expected mood/theme
- Genre focus
- Language(s)
- Ticket price (if applicable)
- Expected audience size

## 🎭 ART FORMS (4 Primary Categories)

### 1. **MUSIC** 
#### Styles:
- **Traditional/Indigenous**: Virindu, Vannam, Kolam Songs, Nadagam Music, Raban Music, Jana Kavi, Harvest Songs, Ritual/Tovil Music, Sanni Yakuma Music
- **Classical/Semi-Classical**: Sinhala Classical, Carnatic Sri Lankan Tamil, Light Classical, Classical Vocal, Classical Instrumental
- **Baila**: Traditional Baila, Modern Baila, Party Baila
- **Sinhala Commercial**: Sinhala Pop, Dance Pop, Acoustic Pop, Ballads, Love Songs, Sad Songs
- **Tamil Commercial**: Tamil Pop, Tamil Melody, Tamil Rap, Gaana-style
- **Hip-Hop/Rap**: Sinhala Rap, Tamil Rap, Trap, Drill
- **Rock/Alternative**: Sinhala Rock, Tamil Rock, Hard Rock, Alternative Rock, Metal
- **Devotional/Religious**: Buddhist Devotional, Bhakti Songs, Catholic Hymns, Islamic Nasheed
- **Fusion**: Folk Fusion, Classical Fusion, Ethnic Electronic, Traditional Hip-Hop Fusion
- **Film Music**: Film Songs, Background Scores, Orchestral Scores

### 2. **DANCE**
#### Styles:
- **Kandyan Dance** (Udarata Natum): Ves Dance, Naiyandi, Pantheru, Uddekki, Vannam Dance
- **Low Country Dance** (Pahatharata Natum): Kolam Dance, Sanni Yakuma, Devil Dance
- **Sabaragamuwa Dance**: Ritual-based, Drum-centered
- **Tamil Classical**: Bharatanatyam
- **Muslim Cultural Dance**: Wedding Dances, Ceremonial Dances
- **Folk Dance**: Harvest Dances, Village Festival Dances, New Year Dances
- **Contemporary/Modern**: Sri Lankan Contemporary, Interpretative, Theatrical Dance
- **Urban/Street**: Hip-Hop, Breaking, Popping, Locking, K-pop Cover
- **Ballroom/Western**: Waltz, Tango, Salsa, Cha-Cha

### 3. **FILM**
#### Styles:
- **Commercial Cinema**: Mass Action, Romantic Commercial, Comedy Commercial, Family Drama
- **Art/Parallel Cinema**: Realism, Social Realism, Symbolic Cinema, Festival Cinema (influenced by Lester James Peries)
- **Political Cinema**: Civil War Themes, Social Justice, Ethnic Conflict
- **Historical/Period**: Ancient Sri Lanka, Colonial Era, Biographical
- **Religious/Mythological**: Buddhist Films, Jataka Tales
- **War Films**: Civil War Narratives
- **Thriller/Crime**: Detective, Police Procedural
- **Horror**: Supernatural, Psychological
- **Romantic**: Romantic Drama, Romantic Comedy
- **Experimental/Independent**: Avant-Garde, Low-budget Indie

### 4. **DRAMA**
#### Styles:
- **Traditional Theatre**: Nadagam, Kolam Theatre, Sokari
- **Modern Stage Drama**: Social Drama, Literary Adaptation, Political Theatre (influenced by Ediriweera Sarachchandra)
- **Teledrama** (TV): Family Drama, Romance Series, Crime Series, Historical Series, Political Series
- **Musical Drama**: Stage Musicals, Opera
- **Experimental/Avant-Garde**: Physical Theatre, Immersive Theatre
